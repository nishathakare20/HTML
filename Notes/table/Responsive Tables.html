15. Responsive Tables

ðŸ”¹ Problem:

Tables with many columns can overflow the screen on smaller devices (like smartphones), making content hidden or breaking the layout.

Solution:

Wrap the <table> inside a <div> with overflow-x: auto; like this: 

<div style="overflow-x:auto;">
  <table>
    <!-- table content -->
  </table>
</div>
